## Art Direction Notes

Win poster, treasure room:

- Freeze the current `I ROLL & I COLLECT!` treasure-room direction.
- The hero jewel-encrusted die should sit physically in the world with contact shadows, nearby dice occlusion, and believable perspective.
- The dice hills should feel less uniform and less like canyon walls. They should look like they were stacked by different people at different times over many years, with varied pile shapes, density, height, paths, and spill patterns.
- Final freeze note: outside of specific dice-cleanup notes, preserve the image. The hero jewel die should have different valid roll values on each visible side, not copied face layouts. Clean up obvious AI-looking dice artifacts such as deformed dice, impossible pip layouts, smeared pips, melted cubes, and unrealistic side values.

Lose poster, factory:

- Freeze the current `I'M A TRASH COLLECTOR!` factory direction.
- No dice in the image.
- Keep `DAILY QUOTA`.
- Only the cans on the hero conveyor belt should have the prop can logo art.
- Avoid extra signs, especially `another bin for the win`, `daily can production`, and `stamping new bins`.
- Avoid character or mascot treatment for the trash cans. The factory should feel more like a real production line with arcade color and motion.
- Final freeze note: outside of specific robot-arm notes, preserve the image. Robot arms should not repeat copy-paste poses; vary elbow angles, wrist angles, reach heights, and tool orientations while keeping the factory believable.
